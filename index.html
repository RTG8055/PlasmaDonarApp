    Pre-Donation checklist

    You may qualify to donate plasma for coronavirus patients if you meet these specific convalescent plasma and regular blood donation eligibility requirements:

    Age & Weight

    You are at least 17 years old and weigh 50kg.*

    Health

    You are in good health and generally feel well even if you are being treated for a chronic condition.

    COVID History

    You are symptom-free from COVID-19 for at least 14 days.

    Documents

    You have a prior, verified diagnosis of COVID-19 but now are symptom free and fully recovered.


    *Additional weight requirements apply for donors aged 18 years or younger.

    Donation Timeline

    • Fill the Donor Google Form

    • Local medical team will contact you.

    • Provide proof of positive COVID-19 laboratory test result and if possible, a follow up test that is negative. You must also have been symptom free for 14 days.

    • A blood test will be done for screening of safe plasma donation and collected.

    • The collected blood will be transported to the blood bank. Your plasma is now ready to save a life!
