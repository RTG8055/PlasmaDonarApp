Donating Plasma in 4 easy steps


● As a donor, you will have to register on the google form
● You will be contacted by the local medical team to see if you are still interested in donating plasma and explain the precautions and risks in detail.
● You will be required to show the positive COVID-19 laboratory test result and if possible, a follow up test that is negative. Also you must have been symptom free for 14 days.
● The Rotary Challa blood bank van has standard disinfection and sanitization process in place for your safety purposes and will be at your place as per agreed convenient time from ______.
● A blood test will be done to screen you for safe plasma donation.
● The collected blood will be transported to the blood bank. Your plasma is now ready to save a life!
